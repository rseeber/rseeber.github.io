Follow my blog via RSS

RSS is the best way to get updates for websites. You can use a single app of your choosing to follow everything from blogs, news sites, (some) social media posts (Bluesky, Mastodon, Reddit, Tumblr, and YouTube all work with RSS). Heck, you can even follow the status of your FedEx and USPS packages via RSS.

If you'd like to follow my blog via RSS, copy the link below, and paste it into a feed reader of your choice.

But feel free to try other apps too, there's a lot of options!
